After completing the German courses in Uzbekistan and taking care of all travel-connected matters, our longterm partner Kulturakademie Dresden will take over and help the candidates with completing the recognition course for foreign nursing staff.

The students will be taught by the very same teachers they were already in touch with during their courses abroad, which will deepen their relationship even further and you will be able to receive a full picture of their knowledge, personality and needs.

Why is Uzbekistan the best place to look for new colleagues?

Uzbekistan is a beautiful diverse country located in Central Asia. The history goes back more than two thousand years, offering many cultural, geographical and religious attractions. The capital city Tashkent is a modern metropolis, full of educated and talented hard working people, waiting for the opportunity to make their dreams come true, perhaps in Germany..

Why do we think that Uzbekistan is the hottest place for recruiting right now?

The Uzbek educational system is of very high quality, your new colleagues will be able to start working immediately and perform all required tasks properly and responsibly right away.

Uzbekistan is a diverse country. Aside from Uzbek, which is a language very similar to Turkish, most people can also speak Russian and English, which makes the nursing staff be able to understand and treat patients of many different ethnicities.

Originating from Central Asia, Uzbeks are some of the most hardworking, helpful and respectful people in the world, similar like the Filipinos. Your working team will profit from their character.
