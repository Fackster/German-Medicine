Step by step procedure

German courses at ProfITlingua in Tashkent

Complete the high intense courses at our language academy and receive not only a recognized B1/B2 certificate, but mainly actual knowledge of German!

Finding your future employer

We will already connect you with potential employers and help you find the perfect match before you even leave your hometown. This way,you will have a job position secured way in advance and will be able to focus solely on your studies.

Relocation to Germany

We will help you take care of all visa and travel connected matters and assist you with everything from flight tickets to finding accommodation in Germany. We’ll be your anchor on the whole new and exciting journey.

Adaptation courses at KA Dresden

After you have settled into your new home, we will leave you in good hands of our colleagues from the Kulturakademie in Dresden. You will already know some of the teachers from your courses back home, which will make the adaptation courses more pleasant and efficient.

Recognition procedure

The recognition procedure is divided into lectures at the Kulturakademie and paid practice at a medical institution. You can complete the practical part at your future work place and already start getting to know the environment as well as your new colleagues.

Beginning of successful career

Congratulations, you have made it! After having completed the recognition procedure, you can begin your dream career in Germany. Should you require any further assistance, you can always rely on us, as our partnership is for a lifetime. We wish you good luck!

Why is Germany the best place to live and develop a successful career?

Germany is a modern and powerful country located in Central Europe. It is claimed to be one of the most developed places in the world and the second most popular destination for expats. It offers endless work opportunities thanks to the fact that its economy is the largest in Europe.

Why else do we think that Germany is the place to be?

The country offers countless beautiful holiday destinations for your weekend getaways. Should it be skiing in the Alps, sightseeing one of the many fairytale castles or enjoying the view of the North Sea, there is always plenty to see and do.

Germany is called “the land of poets and thinkers” for a good reason. Cultural events such as the Oktoberfest and Christmas markets are phenomenal, besides there are 41 properties in Germany on the UNESCO World Heritage List.

Thanks to the highly developed infrastructure, the efficient government and law enforcement system, Germany ranks as one of the safest countries in the world. Need we say more?

There are many aspects which make Germany a place with a very high standard of living. The salaries are solid, food and services are high quality and affordable, the public transport system is efficient and the rental prices of properties are regulated. In addition to that, the german healthcare system guarantees that everyone has access to healthcare with only a small contribution, as it is mostly a state-run collective system. Wouldn’t you like to be a part of it?
